Privacy Policy — PlaneFlow

Last updated: 14 June 2026

PlaneFlow (“the game”, “we”) is a casual air-traffic-control game. This policy explains what data the game handles and how. We keep it minimal on purpose: the game has no user accounts and asks for no personal information.

What we store

The game stores the following locally on your device (in the browser/app storage), so your progress and preferences persist between sessions:

  • Game progress & settings — unlocked levels, stars, best results, chosen language, sound/vibration and visual-theme preferences.
  • Anonymous gameplay analytics — a randomly generated identifier (not linked to you or any account), session timing, and in-game events such as level start/finish, tutorial steps, settings changes, and error/crash details. Alongside each event we store basic technical context: app version, interface language, platform, and screen size.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or any data that identifies you personally.
  • No precise location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera access.
  • No advertising and no selling of data to anyone.

Where the data goes

In the current version, analytics data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server. If we later enable an analytics service to understand aggregate, anonymous usage (for example, how many players complete the tutorial), we will use it only to improve the game, send only the anonymous events described above, and update this policy and the Google Play “Data safety” information accordingly. We never sell your data.

Children

PlaneFlow is suitable for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention & your control

Because the data is stored on your device, you can erase all of it at any time by clearing the app’s data/storage or uninstalling the game. There is no account to delete.

Security

The game is served over HTTPS. Any analytics that is transmitted in future versions will be sent over encrypted connections.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where required, the Google Play listing.
